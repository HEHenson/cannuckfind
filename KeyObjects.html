2 Organization of Cannuckfind¶

2.1 Aggregation of Responses¶

It bears repeating that the purpose of this framework is not to perform analysis at the individual level. The purpose of this framework is to construct a framework that can support statements in aggregate concerning the different attitudes in different geographical domains.

2.2 Types of Responses¶

One innovation of this framework is to all analysts to use judgement concerning the quality of the response. These judgements can be implemented into the training dataset, then used to predict make predictions on the populations as a whole. In many systems there is an NA or Not Available value. This class expands on this concept.
